What is Pappa Fresh?

Pappa Fresh is a fresh dog food delivery service that uses human-grade ingredients and vet-approved recipes. We deliver customized portions directly to your door across Italy in 24-48 hours.

Benefits:

  • Fresh ingredients
  • Vet-approved recipes
  • Fast delivery
  • Customized portions

How it works:

  1. Calculate your dog's needs with our vet calculator
  2. Choose from 5 premium recipes
  3. Get fresh food delivered to your door
  4. Store in fridge for 3-4 days or freeze for 3 months
